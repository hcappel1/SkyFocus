360 Bridge, Austin TX

The 360 bridge connects the northern and southern sections of the 360 highway. Opened for traffic in 1982 it supports 22,000 vehicles daily and is a main tourist attraction for Austin visitors as it contains an elevated scenic overlook.

Barton Springs, Austin TX

Barton springs is a public natural spring pool located on the grounds of Zilker Park in Austin. It is a popular swimming spot for locals and tourists especially during the hot summer months. The swimming pool is about 3 acres in size and is fed by underground springs. The temperature remains consistently 68-70 degrees year round.

Lady Bird Lake, Austin TX

Lady bird lake borders the downtown portion of Austin from the south. It is a popular destination for locals and visitors for kayaking, paddle boarding, boat tours, and swimming. Occasionally, kayakers will cluster around the inlet to the barton springs pool.

Oasis at Lake Travis, Austin TX

The Oasis restaurant is the largest outdoor restaurant in Texas overlooking Lake Travis. It has been deemed the sunset capitol of Texas as it features stunning views to the west from a 450 foot cliff.
