Barton Springs, Austin TX

Barton springs is a public natural spring pool located on the grounds of Zilker Park in Austin. It is a popular destination for both locals and tourists especially during the hot summer months.

360 Bridge, Austin TX

The 360 bridge connects the northern and southern sections of the 360 highway. Opened for traffic in 1982 it supports 22,000 vehicles daily and is a main tourist attraction for Austin visitors as it contains an elevated scenic overlook.

Lady Bird Lake, Austin TX

Lady bird lake borders the downtown portion of Austin from the south. It is a popular destination for locals and visitors for kayaking, paddle boarding, boat tours, and swimming. Occasionally kayakers will join together into a "kayak party".
