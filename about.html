Who is SkyFocus?

We are three guys who love drones, photography, travelling, and seeing the world from above. Our goal is to bring a sense of adventure and exploration to the walls of your office or home. We offer skyshot prints of different cities, landscapes, and natural environments. All prints are sold through Etsy, so click on the Etsy store link in the nav menu to see purchase options.

Henry

I love flying drones. I am an aerospace engineer with a special interest in building these flying companions to capture every perfect shot while I travel. I began printing pieces for the walls of my apartment and felt my apartment had a sense of adventure and openness. After visiting for a weekend, Chris proposed the idea to share these prints with others. At that moment we said "hell yes" to SkyFocus.
